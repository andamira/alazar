## [0.0.1] - 2023-09-01

### Added
- Add PRNGS: `Mult13P1`, `Xabc`, `XorShift8`, `XorShift8Custom`, `XorShift16`, `XorShift32`, `XorShift64`, `XorShift128`, `XorShift128p`, `Xyza8a`, `Xyza8b`.
- Add optional rand_core dependency for implementing `RngCore` and `SeedableRng`.
